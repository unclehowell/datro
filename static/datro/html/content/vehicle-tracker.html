Tracking

Overview

Your Wave Device has the ability to track your vehicle. Wave is also pioneering Tracking using USSD, a cellular channel which is subscription free.

How it Works

Your Wi-Fi Hotspot gives you an stylish dashboard to track your vehicle. Which can also be accessed remotely via the free remote access app. The unit in the vehicle is fitted with a USSD Sim Card, supplied by Myriad Group. The unit in the vehicle will broadcast the exact location periodicly to your Wave Wi-Fi Hotspot (via Myriad Groups Thingstream.io API).

The Result

While in your home anyone authorised to connect to your Wi-Fi can access the Wave Wi-Fi Hotspot Dashboard and the Tracking Feature, to view your vehicles location and battery level. You can also access the Wi-Fi Hotspot dashboard (and subsequently the Tracking Feature) from your mobile from anywhere in the world using the FREE remote access app.

Get Started

You will need two Wave units to track your vehicle. One connected to your internet router (Wi-Fi Hotspot Mode) and the other installed into your vehicle (Tracking Mode). Wave can be ordered via our products section.
