IPTV Monitoring

Overview

Each Wave® Device can be easily configured to Monitor your IPTV.

How it Works

One Wave® connects to your Wi-Fi Router (Wi-Fi Hotspot Mode) and a second device (Sense) clamps onto the power cables entering your building.
The Device Monitoring your IPTV (Sense) will then send the Data to your Wave® Wi-Fi Hotspot Dashboard.

The Result

As you can see from the images, the IPTV monitor solution is highly intelligent, which makes it simple to set-up. The Sense device (sold seperately) detects fluctuations in your IPTV and uses a collection of other data to determine what the device is that caused the fluctuation. This data is shared via an API to Wave's user interface.

Get Started

Wave® can be ordered via our products section. You will also require a Sense device whioh can be purchased through their official website.

Notes

Currently the data concerning your IPTV is transmitted to your Wave® Wi-Fi Hotspot (via Sense's API) and can be accessed via the IoT menu. However the Sense mobile app user interface is currently being migrated to the Wave® Wi-Fi Hotspot dashboard and during a future update all IPTV information will be visible via the IPTV tab, not the IoT tab. Furthermore, the IPTV feature will be accessible remotely via the remote access app as is already the case with the IoT, camera, media and Tracking tabs
