Home Control

Overview

Your Wave® Device has the ability to control Lights, Switches, Appliances and more. This technology is entitled Internet of Thing (IoT) and this is what makes Wave® a cutting edge technology product.

How it Works

In Wi-Fi Hotspot Mode Wave® enables a number of devices around your home to be controlled via Wi-Fi. You simple scan your Wi-Fi network and Wave® will detect all the IoT devices on your home Wi-Fi network. You can then select a virtual switch or slider or display to represent the IoT device on your home control dashboard. Never has full home control been so simple.

The Result

Now every wireless device with a web browser can control the lights and appliances in your home. And you can access your Dashboard and IoT feature from anywhere with the Free remote access app.

Get Started

Wave® can be ordered via our products section. After unboxing and connecting the device to your ISP issued router, you can immidiately scan your network to see all the devices you are able to control via your Wave® Wi-Fi Hotspot

Notes

For AirConditioning Controls we recommend a product called Sensibo, which converts Infrared into Wi-Fi and vice versa. Your Sensibo online account will provide you an API key. Simply enter this into your Wave® Wi-Fi Hotspot menu and the two systems will integrate automatically. Now when you scan your network via Wave's IoT feature, it will detect your AirConditioning Unit(s).
