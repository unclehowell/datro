Media Center

Overview

Your Wave Device has the ability to manage your media and stream Free IPTV. The Media App supports DLNA, Multi-Room and it retrieves subtitles, album and movie covers automatically.

How it Works

Your Wi-Fi Hotspot gives you an stylish dashboard from which you will arrive onto the media tab by default. The media application is hosted on the device so it can work offline. It can also support multiple devices accessing the media library - at the same time.

The Result

Imagine all your movie and music collection, accessible from any device in your home with a web-browser. From that same media library you can chat with other users around your household and dive deeper into any movie or TV Station with IMDB and EPG data automatically feeding and updating as your media library evolves

Get Started

Wave can be ordered via our products section. We recommend installing a Wave unit into the back of each TV throughout your household to create a Multi-Room Link experience. When Wave is running in Multi-Room Link Mode you can control the TV via your mobile phone, via the web-browser, via the Wi-Fi (very cool).

Notes

Smart TV's have web browsers and so the Wi-Fi Hotspot Menu can be accessed without a second Wave device connected to the TV's. However accessing the web-browser on the TV can be tedious. With Wave your Wi-Fi Hotspot Menu is displayed as soon as you turn the TV on.
