DATRO Investments
==================

The internet (specifically web 3.0) is great, but accessing the internet remains an affordable privilege, courtesy of autocratic and centralized service providers.
DATRO is a consortium of free, publicly available, technology solutions (democratic and decentral in nature) - designed to make internet access a free, human right.

This publications details DATRO's investments:

 - Investments made in DATRO
 - Master shareholders register
 - Investment rounds (seed, startup, series A etc)
 - Base-interest rates (accurals & compounding)
