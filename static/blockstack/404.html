Introduction


DATR© enhances your existing internet and smart home experience, by making everything free, democratic and decentralised. Our technology is free, open-source (General Public Licence) and works in conjunction with your existing service provider and hardware. So there's no requirement for you to change your existing ISP, or router, or pay a fee or sign contracts in order to start enjoying the benefits of DATR©.

The experience of using our technology, all begins with a free wireless access point software upgrade, called Hotspotβnβ©. The upgrade process is fully autonomously, making it simple enough for anyone to perform. The benefits of upgrading are endless. For the first time in history you'll be able to explore and install apps to your wireless access point. Our software and apps empower you to save money on your energy and internet bills each month and unleash features of your wireless access point you never knew existing, such as monitoring and controlling your homes energy and ip cameras, appliances etc (locally or remotely). And it doesn't stop there. Hotspotβnβ© also features a rich new iptv/ media center experience AND powerful multi-room, multi-player gaming solution (with virtual wireless gamepad controllers, accessible via your smartphones web browser).

All this from a free & simple wireless access point software upgrade. To get started simply download Hotspotβnβ© and follow the user guide to upgrade your wireless access points software.
